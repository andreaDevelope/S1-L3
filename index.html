Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cramberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewearn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sileed
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off whith a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Methd

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully abdorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour
